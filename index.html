Analyst + designer, one person

Most analysts can crunch the numbers. Most designers can make things look good. I sit in the gap between the two — exactly where insight either reaches a decision-maker or dies in a spreadsheet.

At Braincandy I run survey and FMCG tracking studies end to end — quotas, wave-over-wave validation, significance testing — then design the decks 20+ clients use to make calls. Equally at home on commercial insights and on environmental research, where my MSc in Conservation gives me the domain language.
