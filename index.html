About

Most analysts can crunch the numbers. Most designers can make things look good. I sit in the gap between the two — which is exactly where insight either reaches a decision-maker or dies in a spreadsheet.

At Braincandy I run survey and FMCG tracking studies end to end — quota management, wave-over-wave validation, significance testing — then design the decks that 20+ clients actually use to make calls. The graphic-design background isn't decoration; it's what gets a finding understood in three seconds.

I work remotely across EU time zones, and I'm equally at home on commercial consumer-insights work and on environmental / sustainability research, where my MSc in Conservation gives me the domain language.
